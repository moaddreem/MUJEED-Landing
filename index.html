The Challenge

The Problem

Higher education programs face challenges in managing and monitoring academic activities, which affects the achievement of intended learning outcomes.

Manual Processes & Limited Tools

Reliance on manual processes and limited digital tools increases the workload on faculty — particularly in coordinating course content, tracking learning outcomes, and preparing accreditation reports.

Accreditation Reporting Burden

Collecting evidence, mapping assessments to learning outcomes, and generating reports for bodies like NCAAA and ABET remains tedious and error-prone without proper tooling.

Grade-Only Assessment View

Traditional assessment methods focus mainly on final grades, which limits visibility into actual learning outcomes achievement and delays identification of academic weaknesses.

Low Progress Visibility

Students lack a comprehensive view of their academic progress beyond GPA, making it difficult to understand their actual standing in learning outcomes and course competencies.

Our Approach

One Platform, Three Integrated Solutions

MUJEED brings together accreditation, curriculum design, and student guidance into a unified system — so every part of the academic process is connected.

TAWTHIQ

Accreditation Automation

Automates the accreditation process — collecting assessment data, mapping to learning outcomes, managing KPI targets, and generating reports for NCAAA and ABET.

  • Performance targets & benchmarks
  • Learning outcome achievement analysis
  • Course report tracking & submission
  • Program-wide compliance dashboards

MANHAJ

Curriculum Design & Analysis

Design and manage curricula by defining course details, linking course-level outcomes to program goals, and organizing the study plan with standards-based document support.

  • Course specification management
  • Outcome alignment & validation
  • Standards document parsing
  • Program course organization

IRSHAD

Student Guidance & Advising

Gives students a dashboard showing GPA, enrolled courses, areas needing improvement, grade distributions, and AI-powered academic advising.

  • GPA & progress dashboard
  • Early warning alerts for weak areas
  • Grade tracking per assignment
  • AI Advisor Chatbot
Capabilities

Built for Academic Excellence

Every feature in MUJEED is designed to make academic data clearer, processes smoother, and decisions smarter.

Course Specifications

Define and manage course details including learning outcomes, topics, assessment methods, and references — with support for standards document parsing.

Outcome Alignment

Link course-level outcomes to program-level goals, connect assessment questions to specific outcomes, and analyze coverage gaps across the study plan.

Analytics Dashboards

Clear dashboards for instructors, quality admins, and coordinators showing grade distributions, outcome achievement, performance metrics, and semester-level trends.

Grade & Report Management

Upload grades, generate course reports, track submission status, and keep dashboards and outcome data current across all sections.

Early Warning System

Automatically identify areas where students are falling behind on expected outcomes, enabling early intervention and targeted academic support.

AI-Powered Guidance

A Curriculum Chatbot for program development recommendations, and an Advisor Chatbot that supports students with academic guidance beyond traditional advising.

Process

How MUJEED Works

A streamlined workflow that connects curriculum setup, assessment tracking, reporting, and student insights.

01

Define & Structure

Program Coordinators set up course specifications, define learning outcomes at course and program levels, and organize the curriculum structure.

02

Teach & Assess

Instructors manage their courses, link assessment questions to learning outcomes, upload grades, and track student performance per section.

03

Analyze & Report

Quality admins monitor performance targets, review outcome achievement across the program, and generate accreditation-ready reports.

04

Track & Guide

Students view their progress, spot areas needing improvement, and receive AI-powered guidance to make better academic decisions.
